About Us

Quimera Tech is a Technology company founded by businessman Carlos Henrique Machado and Electrical Engineer Luiz Henrique Fonseca, with the idea of ​​being a reference in innovation in the Brazilian and international music market.

Our first product, the Quimera System, is a success in the market for bringing the possibility and practicality of guitarists being able to play several timbres on a single guitar, we can thus create their sound identity and simulate the timbre of traditional guitars.

Carlos Henrique Machado and Luiz Henrique Fonseca
